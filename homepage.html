About PAEA

Founded in 1972, the Physician Assistant Education Association (PAEA) is the only national organization representing PA education programs. PAEA works to ensure quality PA education through the development and distribution of educational services and products specifically geared toward meeting the emerging needs of PA programs, the PA profession, and the health care industry.
